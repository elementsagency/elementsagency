Our story

Why we started

I started Elements Agency after seeing the same problem over and over — small businesses with huge potential, held back by a weak or non-existent online presence. A restaurant with amazing food that nobody could find. A builder doing incredible work but losing jobs to competitors with better-looking websites.

The options available to them weren't great. Large agencies were too expensive and too impersonal. Freelancers were hit and miss. DIY tools took time they didn't have. So they either overpaid, got burned, or did nothing.

I wanted to build something different — an agency that combines specialist expertise across web design, SEO and paid marketing, works as a genuine partner to the businesses we serve, and charges prices that actually make sense for small businesses.

We're a remote team of specialists — each one focused on their area. Web designers, developers, SEO specialists, ad managers. We bring the right people to each project and we stay involved until the results are there.

"A website alone isn't enough. A business needs all the pieces working together — design, visibility, marketing — to actually grow online. That's what we build."
What we stand for

The way we work

01

Results over deliverables

We don't measure success by how many pages a website has. We measure it by whether it's bringing in more enquiries, more customers, more revenue. That's what we're accountable for.

02

Everything in one place

A business has many moving parts — website, SEO, ads, email. They all need to work together. We provide everything under one roof so nothing falls through the gaps.

03

Affordable doesn't mean cheap

We believe small businesses deserve high-quality digital work at prices they can actually afford. Fair pricing and excellent work aren't mutually exclusive — we prove that every day.

04

Long-term relationships

We're not interested in one-off projects. We want to grow with our clients — be the team they call when they have a new idea, a new challenge, or a new goal.

05

We don't disappear

Most agencies hand over the files and move on. We stay. After launch, we continue improving, optimising and supporting — because that's where the real results come from.

06

Clear and honest always

No jargon, no hiding behind complicated reports. We tell you exactly what we're doing, why we're doing it, and what it means for your business.
