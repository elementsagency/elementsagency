Common problems we solve

Does any of this sound familiar?

🔍

Not showing up on Google

When someone searches "garage near me" or "MOT [your town]", you don't appear — even though you've been serving local customers for years.

📱

An embarrassing website

Your website looks outdated, doesn't load well on phones and doesn't build the trust customers need before they book their car in.

📸

No gallery of your work

Before-and-after photos, detailing results and restoration work are powerful — but if they're only on your Instagram, most potential customers never see them.

⭐

Not enough Google reviews

Customers trust garages with reviews. If yours are sparse compared to a competitor down the road, they'll call them first — even if your work is better.

📞

Relying only on word of mouth

Most automotive businesses rely on regulars and referrals. But if you want to grow — or fill gaps — you need a way to attract new customers consistently.

🗓️

Quiet periods you can't fill

Certain times of year are quieter for bookings. Without a marketing system, you have no way to proactively fill those gaps.
