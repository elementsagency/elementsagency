Common problems we solve

Does any of this sound familiar?

🌱

Hard to find online

Customers want to buy local but they can't find you. If your business isn't online, they'll end up buying from a supermarket instead.

🛒

No way to sell online

You produce brilliant things but can only sell them at markets or from the farm gate. An online shop opens up new revenue with no limits on reach.

📖

Your story isn't being told

People pay more for food and products they feel connected to. If customers don't know who you are and how you work, they're missing the most powerful thing about you.

📅

Seasonal gaps in income

Some parts of the year are naturally quieter. Digital marketing — email, social ads and SEO — helps you smooth out those gaps and find customers year-round.

📧

No email list or repeat customers

You have loyal customers but no way to stay in touch with them between visits. Online shop turns one-time buyers into regulars who come back season after season.

🏪

Competing against supermarkets

Customers want local but convenience often wins. A good website, fast ordering and a strong story help you compete where it counts.
