Carteo
About the project

Built from nothing. Every part of it.

Carteo is a digital menu product designed specifically for restaurants, cafés and hospitality businesses. Instead of printing and reprinting paper menus, restaurants create a digital menu once — with photos, descriptions and prices — and share it with customers via a QR code on the table.

Menus can be updated instantly at any time, support multiple languages for international guests and work on any device without an app download. It's a simple, professional solution to a problem every restaurant has.

This is our own product — which means we had to do everything a new business needs to do: define the brand, build the website, create the marketing strategy, write the content, set up the ads and grow it from zero. Everything you see on carteo.co.uk was created in-house by Elements Agency.

Our approach

The challenge of building your own product.

Building for a client is one thing. Building your own product means making every decision yourself — with no brief, no existing brand and no existing customers.

The challenge

Launching a digital product from scratch meant defining everything from first principles — what the product does, who it's for, how to position it, what the brand looks and sounds like, how to reach restaurants and what will make them switch from paper menus to digital. Every element had to be created, not adapted.

The solution

We approached Carteo exactly as we would a client brief — with strategy first, then brand, then build. We defined the target audience (independent restaurants and hospitality businesses), built a clean brand identity, created the website, wrote all the copy and set up a full marketing stack to grow it from day one.

What we built

A complete brand and marketing engine.

This wasn't just a website project. Carteo required everything a new business needs to exist, be found and grow.

  • Brand identity — name, logo, typography, colour system and tone of voice
  • Website design and development — clean, conversion-focused, fully responsive
  • Demo creation — working product demo so restaurants can see it before they sign up
  • Full copywriting — every page, every section written from scratch
  • SEO foundations — keyword research, on-page optimisation and site structure
  • Email marketing setup — automated sequences for leads and onboarding
  • Paid advertising — Google and Meta campaigns targeting UK restaurants
  • Social media content — strategy and content creation for Instagram and LinkedIn

What's next

A product built to grow with our restaurant clients.

Carteo isn't just a standalone product — it's the entry point into everything else we offer restaurants. A digital menu opens the door to conversations about websites, SEO, ads and email marketing.

It also serves as proof of what we can do. Every restaurant that uses Carteo sees Elements Agency's work in action every day.

Roadmap
