YK Detailing
About the project

A website that puts a local business on the map

YK Detailing is a professional car detailing service based in Canterbury, offering high-quality cleaning, paint protection and vehicle care. The business focuses on attention to detail and reliable service for local customers.

Without a website, the business relied entirely on word of mouth. They needed a clean online presence that would showcase their work, explain their services clearly, and make it easy for local customers to find them and get in touch.

Our approach

Challenge & solution

Local service businesses win work when they're easy to find and easy to trust. That was the focus from day one.

The challenge

YK Detailing had no online presence — no website, no Google visibility. The challenge was to build something from scratch that would immediately look professional, showcase the quality of their work, and help local customers find and contact the business easily.

The solution

We designed a clean, visual website that leads with the work — before and after photos, service descriptions, and a clear path to contact. Local SEO foundations were set up from day one so the business could start appearing in local searches in Canterbury.

Deliverables

What we delivered

A complete website build from strategy to launch — everything handled by us.

  • Custom website design — visual, modern and built around the quality of the work
  • Services pages — each detailing service explained clearly with pricing guidance
  • Work gallery — before and after photos to showcase results and build trust
  • Mobile-first layout — smooth experience on phones where most local searches happen
  • Click-to-call and WhatsApp — easy for customers to get in touch instantly
  • Local SEO setup — targeting Canterbury searches from day one
  • Google Business profile guidance — to support local map rankings
